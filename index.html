DOCUMENTATION

Game Overview

BASED-FLAPPY is a Web3 competitive Flappy Bird built on the Base blockchain. Players compete in weekly contests for real ETH prizes. Every game adds to your cumulative total score — consistency beats a single lucky run.

How It Works

Contests run Monday 00:00 UTC to Sunday 23:59 UTC. Entry fee: 0.00005 ETH. Your TOTAL SCORE is the sum of all game scores that week. Playing 20 games averaging 15 (total 300) beats 5 games averaging 50 (total 250). Top 2 scorers win 50% and 40% of the prize pool respectively; 10% goes to platform fees.

Gameplay

Tap or press SPACE to flap upward. Navigate through pipe gaps — each pipe passed earns 1 point. Hitting pipes, ceiling, or ground ends the game. Score auto-saves. Gravity pulls the bird down, so timing taps is key. Pipe speed and gap stay constant — pure skill, no random spikes.

Strategy

  • Play consistently across the week, not just Sunday
  • Steady 10-20 point games beat one lucky 50+
  • Find your personal tapping rhythm and stick to it
  • Take short breaks between games to stay sharp
  • Watch the leaderboard to know your target

Blockchain & Fairness

All scores are recorded via Supabase with wallet address verification. Each wallet gets one entry per week — no duplicate accounts. Your wallet is your permanent identity across all future weeks. Only entered players' scores are saved.

Weekly Schedule

Resets every Monday 00:00 UTC. Entries close Sunday 23:59 UTC — no late entries accepted. Previous winners stay visible in the Leaderboard tab for full transparency.
