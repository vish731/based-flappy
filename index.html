🎮 BASED-FLAPPY

Based-Flappy is a Web3 gaming experience on Base Chain. Classic Flappy Bird gameplay meets blockchain — fair, transparent, and rewarding weekly competition.

📋 HOW TO PLAY

1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask/Coinbase)
2. Pay 0.00005 ETH entry fee
3. Tap/click or SPACE to fly
4. Avoid pipes, each pipe = +1 point
5. Submit your high score!

🏆 WEEKLY COMPETITION

New contest every Monday. Join anytime, unlimited attempts. Your best score counts for the week.

💰 PRIZE DISTRIBUTION

🥇 1st Place: 50% of prize pool
🥈 2nd Place: 40% of prize pool
⚡ Platform Fee: 10%

✨ REWARD TRANSFER

Rewards are automatically transferred to the top 2 scorers within 24 hours after the weekly contest ends. Winners receive ETH directly to their wallet.

📱 MULTIPLATFORM

Works on desktop, mobile, tablet. Tap on mobile, click on desktop, or press SPACE.
