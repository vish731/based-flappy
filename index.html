Game Overview

BASED-FLAPPY is a Web3 competitive gaming experience on Base blockchain. Players compete weekly for the highest TOTAL SCORE.

How It Works

Weekly contest runs Monday to Sunday. Entry fee: 0.00005 ETH. TOTAL SCORE = sum of all game scores during the week.

Gameplay

Tap or SPACE to fly. Each pipe = 1 point. Play multiple times to increase total!
