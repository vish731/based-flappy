🎮 BASED-FLAPPY

Based-Flappy is a revolutionary Web3 gaming experience built on Base Chain. Combining the classic Flappy Bird gameplay with blockchain technology, we've created a fair, transparent, and rewarding weekly competition where players can showcase their skills and earn real ETH rewards.

📋 HOW TO PLAY

1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or any Web3 wallet)
2. Pay the entry fee of 0.00005 ETH to join the weekly contest
3. Tap or click (or press SPACE) to make the bird fly upward
4. Navigate through the pipes without touching them
5. Every pipe you pass gives you +1 point
6. The higher your score, the better your position on the global leaderboard

🏆 WEEKLY COMPETITION

A new competition starts every Monday at 00:00 UTC and runs for 7 days. All scores are recorded on-chain via Supabase, ensuring complete transparency. Players can join anytime during the week and submit unlimited attempts to improve their high score. Your best score of the week will be considered for the final ranking.

💰 PRIZE DISTRIBUTION

The total prize pool is calculated as: Number of Players × 0.00005 ETH. At the end of each weekly competition:

🥇 1st Place: 50% of total prize pool
🥈 2nd Place: 40% of total prize pool
⚡ Platform Fee: 10% (for development, server costs, and continuous improvements)

✨ REWARD TRANSFER

Rewards will be automatically transferred to the top 2 scorers within 24 hours after the weekly contest ends. Winners will receive their ETH directly to the wallet address they used to enter the contest. No manual claims needed — we handle everything!

🔒 FAIR PLAY & TRANSPARENCY

All scores are stored in a public database and can be verified by anyone. The game is 100% skill-based — no randomness, no luck. Every player has an equal chance to win based purely on their gaming ability. Join the community, practice your timing, and climb the leaderboard!

📱 MULTIPLATFORM SUPPORT

Based-Flappy works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Whether you're playing on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or using the MetaMask in-app browser, the experience is smooth and optimized. Tap on mobile, click on desktop, or press SPACE — the choice is yours!
