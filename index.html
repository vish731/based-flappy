📚 BASED-FLAPPY DOCUMENTATION

🎮 Game Overview

BASED-FLAPPY is a competitive Web3 gaming experience built on the Base blockchain. Players compete weekly to achieve the highest score in the classic Flappy Bird-style game, with real ETH prizes awarded to top performers. The game combines nostalgic arcade mechanics with modern blockchain technology, creating an engaging play-to-earn ecosystem.

⚙️ How It Works

Each weekly contest runs from Monday to Sunday. Players must pay a small entry fee of 0.00005 ETH to participate, which contributes to the prize pool. The prize pool distribution follows a 50/40/10 split: 50% to the first-place winner, 40% to the second-place winner, and 10% allocated as protocol fees for platform maintenance and development. Scores are tracked on-chain via Supabase integration, ensuring transparency and fairness.

🎯 Gameplay Mechanics

Control your bird by tapping or pressing SPACE to navigate through light blue pipes inspired by Base's branding. Each successfully passed pipe awards 1 point. The game ends upon collision with pipes or the ground. Your highest score during the contest week determines your ranking. The game features smooth physics, responsive controls, and stunning visuals with dark blue cityscapes and dynamic animations.

🔐 Security & Fairness

All entry fees are recorded through smart contract interactions, and scores are immutable once submitted. The leaderboard updates in real-time, allowing players to track their progress against competitors. No centralized entity can alter scores or results, ensuring a trustless gaming environment.

🚀 Future Updates

Upcoming features include NFT bird skins, referral bonuses, daily tournaments, and expanded prize pools. The team is committed to building a sustainable gaming ecosystem on Base, with community feedback driving development priorities.

📞 Support

For technical issues or questions, join our community channels. Ensure your wallet is connected to the Base network for seamless transactions. Happy flapping!
