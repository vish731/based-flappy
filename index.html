🎮 ABOUT BASED-FLAPPY

BASED-FLAPPY is a weekly Flappy Bird contest on Base Chain. Pay a small entry fee, play the game, compete globally, and win ETH prizes!

📋 How to Play:

1. Connect your Base wallet (MetaMask/Coinbase)
2. Pay 0.00005 ETH entry fee
3. Tap/Click or press SPACE to make the bird fly
4. Avoid the green pipes
5. The higher your score, the better your rank!
6. Top 2 winners at the end of the week get ETH rewards

💰 Prize Distribution:

🥇 1st Place: 50% of total prize pool
🥈 2nd Place: 40% of total prize pool
⚡ Platform Fee: 10% (for development & maintenance)

📊 Prize Pool Example:

100 players = 0.005 ETH pool
Winner gets 0.0025 ETH | Runner-up gets 0.002 ETH
