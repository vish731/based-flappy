📚 BASED-FLAPPY DOCUMENTATION

🎮 Game Overview

BASED-FLAPPY is an exciting Web3 competitive gaming experience built on the Base blockchain. Players compete weekly to achieve the highest score in this classic Flappy Bird-style game, with real ETH prizes awarded to top performers.

⚙️ How It Works

Each weekly contest runs from Monday to Sunday. Players pay an entry fee of 0.00005 ETH, which contributes to the prize pool. Distribution: 50% to 1st place, 40% to 2nd place, 10% protocol fees. Scores are tracked transparently on-chain.

🎯 Gameplay Mechanics

Tap or press SPACE to fly. Each pipe passed = 1 point. Game ends on collision. Your highest score determines ranking.

🔐 Security & Fairness

All scores are immutable once submitted. Leaderboard updates in real-time. No centralized entity can alter results.

🚀 Future Updates

NFT bird skins, referral bonuses, daily tournaments, and expanded prize pools coming soon.
