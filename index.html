Game Overview

BASED-FLAPPY is an exciting Web3 competitive gaming experience built on the Base blockchain. Players compete weekly to achieve the highest TOTAL SCORE across multiple games.

How It Works

Each weekly contest runs from Monday to Sunday. Players pay an entry fee of 0.00005 ETH. Your TOTAL SCORE = sum of all game scores you achieve during the week.

Gameplay Mechanics

Tap or press SPACE to fly. Each pipe passed = 1 point. Play multiple times to increase your total!

Why Total Score?

Total Score rewards consistent players! It's not just about one lucky game — it's about dedication and skill over time.
